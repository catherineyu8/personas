Better Brewing Experiences: Understanding User Interaction with Espresso Machine Interfaces

Coffee is a part of my every-day life. If I don't buy an iced vanilla oat latte from a cafe, I am making a homemade one with my espresso machine. How does my drink-making experience differ from that of a barista's? I explored different users' interactions and experiences with espresso machine interfaces by interviewing them and crafting personas based on my observations, ultimately aiming to tell a story of their experience with espresso machines.
