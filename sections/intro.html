Better Brewing

Understanding how users interact with espresso machines by observing them, creating personas, and crafting a story

Goal

Coffee is a part of my every-day life. If I don't buy an oatmilk latte from a cafe, I am making a drink at home with my espresso machine and homemade syrup. How does my drink-making experience differ from that of a barista's? I explored different users' interactions and experiences with espresso machine interfaces by interviewing them and crafting personas based on my observations, ultimately aiming to tell a story of their experience with espresso machines.
