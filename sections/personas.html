Creating Personas

I created two characters based on my observations: one reflects less experienced users mainly using the preset settings to make espresso-based drinks that still taste like they was made expertly in a cafe, the other reflects experienced users who want quickly to produce quality espresso and have a deeper understanding of the complexities that make up a good espresso.

Home Barista

Thinks Feels
  • Did I do this right?
  • I hope I don't mess up again!
  • Why did it still not work?! :(
  • In a rush
  • Frustrated and confused by the complexities that make a good espresso
  • Unsure about their actions
  • Excited by their morning coffee
Says Does
  • Aw man, I didn't do it right again
  • Why did just a little bit of espresso come out?
  • I can't believe my machine stopped working on the day I am late!
  • Watches tutorials on making espresso
  • Compares their daily drink to one from a cafe
  • Uses preset settings that are “good enough” but not necessarily perfect

The home barista is a college student using the espresso machine to quickly and easily get their daily caffeine fix, while still making a delicious drink. They don't understand all of the specific details about the espresso-making process, but want to have the same experience and drink quality as a cafe barista by using the portafilter to pull a high-quality espresso.

Cafe Barista

Thinks Feels
  • This machine is limiting my coffee-making abilities!
  • We're especially busy this morning - I'm glad I can make espresso so fast!
  • Confident in knowing when and why an espresso shot doesn't turn out quite right
  • Stressed during the morning rush when they have to keep on making drinks nonstop
Says Does
  • “Iced oat latte for Emma~”
  • This steam wand sucks - it's weak and takes so long to steam milk
  • I'm never using a home-style espresso machine again - I can't control anything!
  • Weighs the amount espresso grounds used, the amount of espresso extracted, and times how long the extraction takes to be precise
  • Compares their espresso to espresso tastings they've done and know are good

The cafe barista is a middle-aged person who primarily works at a coffee shop. They understand many of the underlying complexities that go into making a good espresso shot, and aim to consistently and efficiently pull high-quality espresso shots.
